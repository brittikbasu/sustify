Know more

Why Sustify

At Sustify, we train differently. We help learn. We live sustainably, promote sustainability, and do business to provide something useful to society. We are digital because it’s a great way to teach and track how well something is being learnt.

Measurable

On a KPI-Dashboard relevant results are analysed, so that learning improvement is shown.

Personalized

Content adapts accordingly to individual learning progress, based on an intelligent algorithm respecting learning methodology of spacing and repetition.

Inclusive

Login with face recognition, multilinguality and audio function make sure, that the entire workforce can be reached and educated.

Interactive

Different game formats ensure, that learning is done in different ways, requiring different types of interaction, which increases retention.

Customizable

Due to the Content Management System, content can easily be adapted to special requirements.

Micro-learning

Content provided in small bites, which is relevant to day-to-day factory life. Therefore we do not use the concept of levels but concentrated content broken down on key issues.

Strong global partnerships and cooperation
