Why Sustify

We believe in a bottom-up approach to ensure compliance beyond auditing. We help to understand, we increase awareness, and we prepare the grounds for change. We are digital because it’s a great way to teach and track how well something is being learnt.

Micro-learning

Content is limited to be relevant to day-to-day factory life, provided in small “bites”. So Micro-learning is a skill based learning and education which deals with relatively small learning units.

Personalized

Content adapts accordingly to individual learning progress, based on an intelligent algorithm respecting learning methodology of spacing and repetition.

Inclusive

Login with face recognition, multilinguality and audio function make sure, that the entire workforce can be reached and educated.

Interactive

Different game formats ensure, that learning is done in different ways, requiring different types of interaction, which increases retention.

Customizable

Due to the Content Management System, content can easily be adapted to special requirements.

Measurable

The KPI Dashboard displays demographic data, monitors the attendance of users and analyzes results (anonymized averages).
