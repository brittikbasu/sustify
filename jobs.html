Working at Sustify

We are looking for people that are a fit. A fit that works both ways. We live by our values instead of only talking about them. Honesty, reliability and sustainability are important to us. We believe in supporting one another in working together towards a common goal.
We are a learning organisation and think in goals, not hierarchies. Non-violent communication, design thinking and systemic consensing are important elements of our work. If you would like to know more, get in touch. We’d love to hear from you.
