Internship: Instructional design

(Berlin)

You like to craft content and help to develop creative storyboard content. You are familiar with multimedia, are creative and have strong verbal communication skills. You think sustainability is a value and not a hobby and live it yourself. You think out of the box and like experimenting. Skills & Requirements


    • Digital native, familiar with Microsoft office and ideally other programs such as Photoshop, Figma/Indesign and Videoscribe

    • Fluency in English, an additional Asian language, such as Hindi, Mandarin, Bangla or Urdu is a plus.

    • Enrollement as a student in a bachelor or master program in media design, sustainability, or IT-related subjects.
